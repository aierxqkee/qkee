Female Russian Figure Scating

Team Tutberidze is the name of the group of figure skaters coach Eteri Tutberidze in the "Crystal" department of the Sports School "Sambo-70."

Names of the coaches City of Origin
Eteri TUTBERIDZE Georgia, Tbilisi
Daniil GLEIKHENGAUZ Mockow, Russia
Sergei DUDAKOV Mockow, Russia

Among those brought up by the Honored coach of Russia are two-time champion of Russia, the world and Europe, twice Olympic silver medalist Evgenia Medvedeva, European Championship medalist Sergey Voronov, Olympic champions Yulia Lipnitskaya and Alina Zagitova. Under Tutberidze's guidance, Anna Shcherbakova, the first performer of two quad lutz in the program, grew up, Alexandra Trusova was the first in the world to master the 4-3 cascade.
